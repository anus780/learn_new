The following computer algorithms are based on the divide and conquer programming approach:-

  • Merge sort
  • Quick sort
  • Binary search
  • strassen's Matrix Multiplication
  • Selection sort
  • Min max

There are various ways available to solve any computer problems,but the mentioned are a good examples of divide and conquer methods.
